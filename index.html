Welcome To PSR Automation

Incorporated in 2020, **PSR Automation** is a leading manufacturer and supplier of **Surface Treatment Machines**. We specialize in the design and supply of automatic, semi-automatic, and manual electroplating systems customized for industrial surface finishing needs. Our advanced equipment caters to a wide range of processes, including: Alkaline Zinc Plating, Soft & Hard Anodising Process, Chromodising Process, Alodine Process, Tri Nickel Chrome Plating Process, Plating on Plastic (POP) Process, Phosphating Process, and CED Coating Process.

About Us

We are a manufacturer of the Industrial Chemical Processing lines, plants, and systems like Plating, Anodizing, etc., along with paint booths and various treatment equipment. The conceptualization of design and master planning is done in such a way that it meets the Standard Operating Procedures without any harm to the environment. It largely involves preparing the ground work for plant layout, manpower estimation, and KPIs being the major parameters among others.

**QUALITY:** We follow a systematic approach for projects that demand high infrastructural needs. Our team of expert engineers drafts a framework keeping in mind the expectations of the clients as well as the budget. The emphasis is laid on setting up an Electroplating plant with proper planning and a strong substratum.
